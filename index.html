I’ve always been a creative and empathetic person with a strong desire to help others. Prior to becoming a web developer and designer, I worked in non-profit organizations focusing on animal welfare, childcare, and accessibility for the Deaf and hard of hearing. After I received my diploma in Graphic Design, I went on to complete Juno College’s Web Immersive Bootcamp where I learned to design and build accessible and responsive web applications independently and collaboratively using HTML, CSS, and Javascript as well as other front-end technologies. My top skills are conflict resolution, embracing diversity and communication.
